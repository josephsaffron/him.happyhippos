serenaded

I think one of the best ways to be woken up is to have someone sing you awake. I mean, wouldn't it be amazing to be serenaded awake every day? That would be the best alarm clock ever! Well, today I was awoken (albeit unintentionally) by someone singing. It turned out to be Jasy practicing for her audition, but still, it was enjoyable. =) She was singing “His Eye is on the Sparrow”, a really touching song that I first heard at KWCAC, sung by Clara. The lyrics really drive home the point that we don't have to worry or be anxious about anything, since God is watching over our every action. I don't think Jasy caught the irony in her worrying about her audition while singing this song. I think that you'll appreciate the lyrics though:

Why should my heart be lonely, and long for heaven and home,
